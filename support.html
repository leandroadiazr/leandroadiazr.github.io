About the App

Flip & Hold Analyzer is a professional real estate underwriting tool built for:

  • Real Estate Investors
  • Realtors working with investor clients
  • Deal finders / wholesalers
  • Private lenders

The app helps analyze:

  • Fix & Flip projects
  • BRRRR (Refinance & Hold) strategies
  • Multi-Family & Small Hotel investments

Core Features

Single Family Analysis
  • Profit & ROI on Cash
  • Max Allowed Offer calculation
  • Hard Money Loan (HML) modeling
  • Deal Score (Strong / Borderline / Pass)
  • BRRRR refinance & DSCR projections
Multi-Family / Hotel Analysis
  • NOI & Cap Rate calculations
  • Operating expense modeling
  • Debt service & DSCR metrics
  • Investor contribution tracking
  • Pro Forma vs Actual performance comparison
AI Property Analyzer (Offline)
  • Paste wholesale or MLS deal descriptions
  • Auto-detect purchase price, ARV, rehab, rent
  • Generate comparable sales (synthetic)
  • Confidence scoring
  • Risk flag detection (structural, financing, etc.)

How to Use

  1. Select Single Family or Multi-Family deal type.
  2. Enter deal numbers manually or use the AI Analyzer to pre-fill data.
  3. Adjust underwriting assumptions conservatively.
  4. Review ROI, Deal Score, and projections.
  5. Save viable deals for comparison and export PDF summaries.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is ROI calculated?

ROI is calculated as projected net profit divided by total cash invested (HML-adjusted if enabled).

What is Deal Score?

Deal Score is a weighted evaluation based on profitability, ROI, max offer compliance, and ARV validation.

Is the AI connected to the internet?

No. The AI Analyzer runs locally on your device using structured parsing logic.

Where is my data stored?

All deal data is stored locally on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework. No external servers are used.

Data & Privacy

Flip & Hold Analyzer does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. All information entered into the app remains on your device. We do not sell or share user data.

Technical Information

  • Built natively for iOS using SwiftUI
  • Local data storage via SwiftData
  • Offline AI-style deal parsing engine
