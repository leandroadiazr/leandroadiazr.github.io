SocialMedia is an iOS app built with SwiftUI that allows users to share, interact, and engage with audio content such as voice notes, podcasts, and audio diaries. It integrates features commonly found in popular social media platforms like Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram, and X.

  • Like, comment, and share: Interact with audio content similar to Facebook and Instagram.
  • Real-time messaging and status updates: Features similar to WhatsApp.
  • Follow and discover trending content: Functions similar to X.
  • Explore content based on interests: Curated feeds tailored to user preferences.
  • Light/Dark mode support: Enhanced viewing experience for all users.
